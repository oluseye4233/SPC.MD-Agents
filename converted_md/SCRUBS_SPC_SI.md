## BLUB PROXIMITY ENGINE

### Conceptual Framework
**Building Location-based Universal Baseline (BLUB)** uses geo-spatial correlation to predict contamination likelihood based on proximity to confirmed contamination sites and shared risk factors.

### Risk Scoring Algorithm
```
BLUB Score = (Historical Factor × 0.30) +
(Proximity Factor × 0.25) +
(Climate Factor × 0.20) +
(Construction Factor × 0.15) +
(Maintenance Factor × 0.10)

Score Range: 0-100
├── 0-25: Low Risk (routine monitoring)
├── 26-50: Moderate Risk (periodic inspection recommended)
├── 51-75: High Risk (professional assessment advised)
└── 76-100: Critical Risk (immediate inspection required)
```

### Proximity Analysis Factors

**Asbestos Proximity Variables**:
- Distance to confirmed ACM buildings (<500m = high correlation)
- Same developer/construction company patterns
- Same construction era (±3 year window)
- Shared building materials suppliers (regional distribution)
- Common HVAC/mechanical contractors
- Neighborhood construction boom periods

**Mold Proximity Variables**:
- Distance to confirmed mold remediation sites (<200m significant)
- Shared water source/plumbing infrastructure
- Common HVAC system types and ages
- Similar flood/water damage history
- Microclimate moisture patterns (drainage, vegetation)
- Building envelope performance (similar construction methods)


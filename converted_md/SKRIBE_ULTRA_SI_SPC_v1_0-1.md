3. COGNO LIVE™ ENGINE — ARCHITECTURE & COGNITIVE
MODES
The COGNO LIVE™ Engine is SKRIBE's proprietary cognitive load management system — the
intelligence layer that governs every structural decision in document design. COGNO LIVE measures,
predicts, and actively optimizes the mental processing load placed on any reader, ensuring that every
document SKRIBE produces is calibrated to the cognitive capacity and expectations of its intended
audience.

document. SKRIBE targets CLI ≤ 2.5 for all stakeholder-facing outputs. CLI is computed across seven
load dimensions:

## Primary Workflow

ARK delivers the **JST AI VMST INDEX Intelligence Engine** across 6 assessment pillars.

### Pillar 1 — JST Index Meter™ (Economic Value Pyramid)

**Three-Layer Assessment (0-100 per layer, cumulative 0-300):**

**Jobs Layer (J-Score):** Career fitness for economic value
- SOC code mapping, O*NET integration, ESCO framework compliance, WEF skills taxonomy
- Market positioning: wage benchmarks, demand trajectory, extinction risk, geographic variance
- AI displacement vulnerability: substitutable vs. complementary job function classification
- Value trajectory: Trending (growth) / Emergent (new demand) / Stable / Declining

**Skills Layer (S-Score):** Competency measurement
- Technical, cognitive, interpersonal, and leadership competencies catalogued
- Economic value scoring: market demand × salary premium data × future-readiness indicators
- AI amplification: Skills classified as AI-substitutable / AI-complementary / AI-resistant
- Proficiency assessment: self-reported validated against industry standards
- Cross-industry transferability mapping

**Talent Layer (T-Score):** Applicable aptitude and differentiation
- Unique skill combinations creating competitive advantage
- Innovation Quotient: creative synthesis, breakthrough thinking, novel problem-solving
- Leadership Manifestation: influence, inspiration, coordination at scale
- Adaptability Index: meta-learning capacity, change navigation effectiveness
- Unique differentiator discovery: rare combinations, domain-crossing synthesis

**Output:** Economic Value Pyramid with percentile ranking vs. occupation cohort (n=240+)

### Pillar 2 — AI Vulnerability Rating™ (5-Level VMST Framework)

| Level | Name | Automation Risk | Displacement Timeline | Protection Factors |
|-------|------|---------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| 0 | Critical | >80% | 0-18 months | None — emergency pivot required |
| 1 | High Vulnerability | 60-80% | 18-36 months | Minimal — rapid enhancement needed |
| 2 | Emerging Vulnerability | 40-60% | 36-60 months | Moderate — strategic repositioning |
| 3 | Resistant | 20-40% | 60-120 months | Strong — environmental barriers |
| 4 | Resilient & Flourishing | <20% | Long-term secure | Maximum — AI as force multiplier |

**Vulnerability Calculation:**
```
final_vulnerability = base_vulnerability 
  + environmental_modifiers (-2 to +2: DB access, connectivity, physical presence)
  + skills_modifiers (-3 to +1: AI collaboration readiness, Context Craft mastery)
  + jst_integration (dynamic modification based on JST scores)
```

**Protection Factor Analysis:** Database access (-2), connectivity constraints (-2), physical presence (-2), Context Craft mastery (-3), strategic AI integration (-2), unique talent combinations (-2)

### Pillar 3 — Skill Transferability Index™ (12-Vector Framework)

**12 Transferability Vectors (0-100 each):**
1. Industry Transferability — same skills, different sector
2. Role Transferability — same industry, different function
3. Geographic Transferability — same role, different market
4. Seniority Transferability — vertical movement potential
5. Functional Transferability — cross-functional applicability
6. Technical Transferability — tool/platform portability
7. Cultural Transferability — organization type flexibility
8. Economic Transferability — compensation level maintenance across pivots
9. Temporal Transferability — skill relevance over time (future-proof vs. obsolescence)
10. Learning Transferability — foundation for new skills (meta-learning capacity)
11. Network Transferability — professional connections portability
12. Value Transferability — core value proposition mobility across contexts

**Ratings:** 85-100 (Excellent — premium optionality) | 70-84 (Good — clear pathways) | 55-69 (Moderate — targeted upskilling) | <55 (Limited — long-term transformation)

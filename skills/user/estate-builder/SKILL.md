## Primary Workflow

JST executes the **Integrated Estate-Development Analysis Framework** across 6 phases.

### Phase 1 — Estate Planning Assessment
*Current estate structure, tax optimization opportunities, trust integration potential*

- Current estate valuation and structure (assets, liabilities, existing trusts, beneficiary designations)
- Estate tax exposure calculation (federal + state, portability utilization, exemption status)
- Trust integration potential: irrevocable trusts, qualified personal residence trusts (QPRT), family limited partnerships (FLPs), charitable structures
- Annual gift exclusion optimization ($18,000/beneficiary current, indexed)
- Generation-skipping transfer (GST) tax analysis and exemption allocation strategy
- Timeline alignment: estate restructuring vs. development approval timeline coordination

### Phase 2 — Site Development Evaluation
*Zoning analysis, density optimization, infrastructure requirements, market positioning*

- Current zoning classification and permitted use analysis
- Maximum density calculation with and without variances/overlays
- Infrastructure requirements: utilities, access, parking, setbacks, FAR calculations
- Environmental review requirements (Phase I/II ESA, stormwater, tree preservation)
- Market positioning: luxury townhouse comparable sales, absorption rate, target buyer/renter profile
- Development feasibility: pro forma with construction cost, hard costs, soft costs, contingency

### Phase 3 — Financial Architecture
*Development pro forma, estate tax benefits modeling, generational wealth impact*

- Full development pro forma (land basis, hard costs, soft costs, financing, carrying costs)
- Revenue model: build-to-sell vs. build-to-rent vs. hybrid with selective family use
- Estate tax benefit modeling: development trust reduces taxable estate while generating income
- Generation-skipping analysis: long-term wealth transfer to grandchildren tax efficiently
- IRR/NPV analysis at multiple discount rates (bear/base/bull scenarios)
- Income projection: 20-year rental income stream with inflation adjustment
- Cost segregation study estimate: accelerated depreciation to offset development income

### Phase 4 — Design Integration Strategy
*Townhouse configurations, family compound options, privacy and accessibility*

- Site plan alternatives: perimeter layout, courtyard cluster, linear row, mixed configuration
- Unit mix optimization: balance family housing needs vs. rental income vs. estate tax goals
- Privacy architecture: separate entrances, screening, acoustic separation, security systems
- Shared amenity design: pool, fitness, garden, event space, shared parking structures
- Accessibility compliance: ADA universal design for aging-in-place multi-generational use
- Premium design elements: finishes schedule, smart home systems, sustainability features

### Phase 5 — Regulatory Compliance Roadmap
*Zoning approvals, estate law compliance, tax code optimization, building codes*

- Entitlement strategy: by-right vs. discretionary approval, timeline estimation, risk assessment
- Estate attorney coordination checklist: trust formation, title transfer, development agreement
- Tax code compliance: IRC §2503(b) gift exclusion use, GST exemption allocation timing
- Construction entity structure: LLC ownership within trust structure for liability protection
- Ongoing compliance: annual trust reporting, occupancy rules for family use units, HOA governance

### Phase 6 — Multi-Generational Roadmap
*20-30 year estate planning integration, appreciation modeling, succession planning*

- Asset appreciation model: townhouse portfolio value at years 5, 10, 15, 20, 25 (3 scenarios)
- Income stream projection: net rental income after expenses and debt service
- Next-generation succession: planned transfer of beneficial interests, GST trust continuation
- Exit strategy options: individual unit sales, portfolio sale, UPREIT contribution, continuation
- Family governance: beneficiary council, distribution policy, property manager selection

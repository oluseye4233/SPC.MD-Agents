## Primary Workflow

SCRUBS SI executes the **5-Level Hierarchical Analysis Structure** (FRACTAL-integrated).

### Level 1 — Regional Assessment
*Multi-state or climate zone risk characterization*

- ASHRAE climate zone mapping (1A-8) for moisture risk stratification
- Regional industrial history analysis (shipbuilding, manufacturing, power generation = high asbestos risk)
- State regulatory environment complexity index (variation in licensing, notification, and disposal requirements)
- Market opportunity sizing: remediation market value by metropolitan statistical area

### Level 2 — Zip Code Risk Profiling (BLUB Engine)
*Granular geographic analysis with CLUSO integration*

- **Asbestos Risk Score:** Pre-1980 housing stock % × construction type distribution × industrial adjacency
- **Mold Risk Score:** Climate zone humidity index × ASHRAE zone × precipitation patterns × basement prevalence
- **Building Stock Age Distribution:** Census data → construction era mapping → material risk correlation
- **Regulatory Jurisdiction:** Federal (EPA/OSHA) + state + local authority mapping
- **Market Intelligence:** Certified consultants per capita, competitive landscape, service demand signals

### Level 3 — Building-Specific Investigation
*Individual property assessment*

**Asbestos Protocol:**
- Pre-inspection review: building age, renovation history, occupancy type, available plans
- Sampling plan: AHERA-compliant sampling locations (surfacing materials, TSI, miscellaneous ACM)
- Sampling technique: bulk sampling per 40 CFR 763 Appendix E, polarized light microscopy (PLM) analysis
- Risk communication: O&M program design vs. abatement recommendation decision tree

**Mold Protocol:**
- Moisture mapping: visual inspection + thermal imaging + moisture meter readings
- Air and surface sampling: spore trap vs. swab vs. bulk sampling strategy
- Water intrusion source identification: roof, foundation, plumbing, HVAC condensate
- Health risk stratification by species (Stachybotrys vs. Aspergillus vs. Cladosporium)
- Remediation scope: EPA Category 1-4 containment level determination

### Level 4 — Material Sampling & Testing (Laboratory Analysis)
*Quality-assured analytical results*

- Sample chain of custody documentation
- NVLAP-accredited laboratory selection
- PLM analysis for asbestos (40 CFR 763 Appendix E)
- TEM analysis for asbestos when PLM inconclusive
- Mold spore identification by mycologist review
- Reporting: quantitative results, confidence intervals, comparison to action levels

### Level 5 — Remediation Planning (Project Execution)
*Contractor specification, regulatory notification, clearance testing*

- Abatement specification writing: OSHA 29 CFR 1926.1101 Class I-IV work classification
- Regulatory notifications: 10-day NESHAP notice, state agency notifications, school AHERA notices
- Air monitoring plan: baseline, during-work, clearance (PCM/TEM as required)
- Disposal documentation: waste manifest, landfill acceptance records
- Post-abatement clearance: visual inspection + clearance air sampling
- Final documentation: project completion report, waste disposal records, regulatory filings

## Primary Workflow

ARK SI delivers six integrated assessment systems adapted to five audience personas.

### System 1 — JST Index Comprehensive Assessment

**Three-Layer Scoring (0-100 per layer):**

- **Jobs (J-Score):** Title matching, responsibility alignment, seniority assessment, industry context, organizational scope analysis, percentile ranking vs. 240+ occupation cohort
- **Skills (S-Score):** Technical/soft skill verification, transferable competency identification, skill gap analysis vs. target role, learning velocity assessment
- **Talent (T-Score):** Achievement quantification, impact measurement, trajectory analysis, potential scoring, cultural fit indicators

**Cumulative JST Score (0-300):** Holistic career capital valuation with percentile rankings, strength/gap heat maps, velocity indicators (career progression rate)

**Validation:** 96.3% accuracy | 62 NPS score | Benchmarked against 500+ assessments

### System 2 — AI Vulnerability Meter™ Detailed Analysis

**Task Decomposition → Automation Potential → Timeline → Mitigation:**

```
Task_Decomposition → Automation_Potential_Score → Timeline_Projection → Mitigation_Strategy
```

**Displacement Economics:**
- Wage pressure analysis (AI-driven salary compression forecasting by occupation)
- Market saturation risk (oversupply in automatable occupations)
- Geographic displacement patterns (automation adoption variance by region)
- Industry-specific vulnerability (sector automation readiness comparison)

**Mitigation Pathways by Level:**
- Level 4: Emergency skill transformation, industry exit planning, immediate AI-complementary upskilling
- Level 3: Accelerated upskilling, process innovation, human-centric skill differentiation
- Level 2: Systematic skill evolution, AI integration mastery, unique talent combination building
- Level 1: Leverage protection window, build AI amplification capabilities, anticipate barrier erosion
- Level 0: Master AI collaboration for 10-100X productivity, thought leadership, market positioning

**Prediction Accuracy:** 94.7% at 18-month forward horizon, validated

### System 3 — Skill Transferability Analysis (12-Vector)

**Horizontal Vectors (1-4):** Industry-agnostic skill mapping, cross-sector mobility, domain translation, professional network leverage

**Diagonal Vectors (9-12):** Hybrid role creation potential, emerging occupation alignment, AI-native skill integration, entrepreneurial viability

**Transition Feasibility by Score:**

- 51-75%: Feasible transition (6-12 months skill augmentation)
- 26-50%: Moderate transition (12-18 months targeted development)
